Entrepreneurship

I believe that theoretical and technological innovation in engineering should be committed to industrialization. We established the ClearTactile (清触未来) tech startup team in May 2022 and gradually gained widespread recognition [product Introduction in Chinese]. The predecessor of our team is an undergraduate research interest group at Tsinghua University. In April 2022, we were honored to receive the only Grand Prize in Tsinghua Craftsmanship Awards. It is one of the highest-level entrepreneurship competitions at Tsinghua University that was widely reported by mainstream media in China, including the report of Xinhua News with over 1.3 million views [news report in Chinese]. As the representative, I received the award from the principal of Tsinghua University, Xiqin Wang [news report in Chinese].

Me and the Former Principal of Tsinghua University

At present, ClearTactile has competed with mature teams of graduate students many times and repeatedly won remarkable results.

  • 2024 May: Team of Excellence, 2024 China-U.S. Young Maker Centers Annual Conference
  • 2023 Jun: Grand Prize in the 2023 “Challenge Cup” Capital Science and Technology College of Extra-curricular Academic Competition, Special Track of Cool Tech
  • 2023 Mar: 4th Place in the “Kunshan Cup” 24th Tsinghua University Entrepreneurship Competition [news report in Chinese]
  • 2022 Dec: Bronze Award in the 8th China College Students’ “Internet+” Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, National Finals [news report in Chinese]
  • 2022 Sep: 3rd Place in the special invitation track of the 4th “Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei and Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao” Youth Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition
  • 2022 Jul: 1st Prize in the 8th China College Students’ “Internet+” Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, Beijing Area 07/2022
  • 2022 Jun: The top ten teams in the China College Students’ “Internet+” Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, Tsinghua University, 2022 (The only undergraduate student team)

The ambition of ClearTactile is to build a platform that can fully mobilize, cultivate, and bring into play the talents of undergraduate students. We are actively exploring the possibilities for undergraduate research and encouraging more undergraduates to engage in academic research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
